Frequently Asked Questions üí≠

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply create an account, browse our courses, and click "Enroll Now" on any course page. You'll get instant access to all course materials.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium courses. All our foundational courses are completely free to access.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon completing a course and passing the final quiz, you'll receive a certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and other platforms.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely! All our courses are self-paced, so you can learn whenever and wherever works best for you.

What if I need help with a course?

We have a community forum where you can ask questions, and our support team is always ready to help via email.

What prerequisites do I need?

Prerequisites vary by course. Beginner courses require no prior knowledge, while advanced courses may require basic programming and math skills.
